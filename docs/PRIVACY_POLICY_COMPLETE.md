## What You Need to Do

### 1. Customize the Privacy Policy

Edit `PRIVACY_POLICY.md` and replace:
- `[Date]` with actual dates
- `[Your Contact Email]` with your email
- `[Your Website URL]` with your website (if applicable)
- `[Your Business Address]` with your address

### 2. Host the Privacy Policy

Choose one of these options:

**Option A: GitHub Pages (Recommended - Free)**
- Upload `PRIVACY_POLICY.md` to a GitHub repository
- Enable GitHub Pages in repository settings
- Your URL will be: `https://username.github.io/repo-name/PRIVACY_POLICY.md`

**Option B: Your Website**
- Upload to your website
- Accessible at: `https://yourwebsite.com/privacy-policy`

**Option C: Privacy Policy Generator**
- Use a service like Termly or FreePrivacyPolicy.com
- Copy content from `PRIVACY_POLICY.md` as reference

### 3. Submit to Google Play Console

When publishing:
1. Go to Play Console → Your App → Policy → App content
2. Find "Privacy Policy" section
3. Enter your hosted privacy policy URL
4. Save and submit
